About Me

For the past 5 years I was an Automotive enthusiast. I am fascinated by the engineering minds that build every detail in our modern cars and have them work together for years. An engine or a car seat, the transmission or the windshield wipers, everything was designed to serve us.

Fortunately, today we have way more engineers which will build not only cars but also small apps on our phones to help us perform more efficient. I do have my very strong intention to become one of them and help build a better future for all of us!
